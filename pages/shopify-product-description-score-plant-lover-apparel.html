What a Shopify description needs that an Etsy one doesn't

On Shopify there's no marketplace search carrying the buyer the last mile — your description does more of the selling. Lead with the benefit, break into scannable bullets, and always include material, fit, size chart and care. The score rewards exactly these, and flags walls of generic text.

  • Bullets > paragraphs for specs
  • Name the buyer ("for the plant mom who has every pot")
  • Close with an FAQ to remove doubt
