What a strong gardening listing looks like

  • Title: use the full 140 characters, lead with the buyer's phrase ("Gardening Shirt"), then stack long-tail ("Plant Lover Gift, Botanical Tee, Gift for Gardener").
  • Tags: fill all 13, favor multi-word phrases, keep each ≤20 chars.
  • Description: scannable bullets — material, fit, size chart, care, gift use.
  • FAQ: 3–5 questions on sizing, shipping and care cut pre-purchase hesitation.
  • Differentiation: in-group language ("propagation station", "crazy plant lady") beats generic "cute gardening shirt".
